# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General

**Q1. Is this a medical or diagnostic test?**
> **No.** This application is not a medical or psychological diagnostic tool. It is meant for **self-reflection and educational purposes only**.

**Q2. Are my responses stored?**
> User responses may be stored **securely** to improve insights and future features. Personal data is handled responsibly and with user consent.

**Q3. Can I retake the exam?**
> **Yes.** Users can retake the exam to track changes in their emotional patterns over time.

**Q4. How are the results calculated?**
> Results are currently generated based on predefined logic. In future updates, we may utilize data-driven or ML-based analysis.

**Q5. Can I edit my personal details later?**
> **Yes.** Users will be able to update their profile information as new features are added.

**Q6. Will this app give therapy or treatment advice?**
> **No.** The app may provide general insights or suggestions, but it **does not replace professional help**.

**Q7. Is my data shared with others?**
> **No.** Personal data is never shared without consent. Any data used for analysis is fully anonymized.

**Q8. Who should I contact for support or feedback?**
> You can raise an issue on our [GitHub repository](LINK_HERE) or contact the project maintainers through our community channels.
